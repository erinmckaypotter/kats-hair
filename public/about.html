Kat's Hair Design

About

Kat Sauvage has been designing hair for 45 years. She studied cosmotology in Chicago, Il, and has worked in well-known salons in Chicago, Las Vegas, and San Fransisco. She also participated in several hair shows in SF as a professional colorist throughout the 80's.

Kat owned her own salon on Gough St. in San Fransico for 20 years, and currently works out of her scenic home studio in Healdsburg, CA.
